Hi! I'm BundleBob.

Your bundle setup sidekick.

Resident helper at BundleBoss. I look after shops that sell things which come in parts. This is my story.

Where I come from

Look closely at the BundleBoss logo and you'll find me. The crown became my head; the open box became my body. The founder drew a logo, not a mascot, then noticed it was looking back. I've been here ever since.

What I do all day

I keep an eye on builds. Every part picked, every rule checked, every price honest to the penny. If one piece doesn't fit another, that combination never reaches your cart. That's the job, and I take it seriously.

What I think about

Impossible combinations, mostly. Right now, somebody somewhere is about to bundle a left shoe with two right ones. Not on my watch.

My favourite moment

When the last slot clicks into place and the total settles. A finished build is a small, perfect thing. You made it. I just kept the rails on.

When things go wrong

Sometimes they do. I feel terrible about it, and then we fix it. Email support@getbundleboss.com and a human reads it. No bots, no runaround. I'm a mascot, not a chatbot. There's a difference.

Want me in your shop?

BundleBoss is the bundle builder WooCommerce deserves: a genuinely useful free plugin and one Pro tier for the Builder. I come included with both.
