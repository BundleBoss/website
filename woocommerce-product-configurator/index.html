Product configurator

Let customers configure the bundle,
not just buy it.

BundleBoss turns a WooCommerce product into a guided configurator. Shoppers choose the variation for each item, see the running total update to the penny, and add the whole thing to the cart as one line.

See pricing How it works

What the customer does

Pick each variation

Size, colour, finish, whatever your products use. Only the combinations you stock are offered, shown as tappable swatches rather than dropdowns.

Watch the price move

The total updates live as choices change, with any bundle discount already applied. No surprises at the cart.

Buy it as one thing

The configured bundle adds to the cart as a single line and travels through checkout and the order as one unit, with every choice recorded.

What you set up

Set the product type to Bundle, add the items, and pick how they are laid out: a simple list, a grid, or a step-by-step flow. For variable products bought in quantity, the quantity grid lets a shopper type a whole size run in one place. That is the configurator, no code.
