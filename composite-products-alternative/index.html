The three you would buy

To match what BundleBoss Pro does using official WooCommerce.com extensions, you would buy Product Bundles (about £60 a year), Composite Products (about £112) for build-your-own, and Product Add-Ons (about £49) for personalisation. That is roughly £220 a year, across three separate plugins. BundleBoss Solo is £79 and built as one.

Why one plugin matters

Three plugins means three settings screens, three things to update, three places to ask for help, and the edge cases where they do not quite talk to each other. BundleBoss is one tool built to do bundling, build-your-own and personalisation together. And it personalises every unit on its own, a different name on each shirt, which the three are not built to do even side by side.

The official extensions are good and well supported. If you only need one piece, they are a fine choice. BundleBoss is for when you want the whole configure-and-personalise flow as a single, cheaper thing.
