## The two halves of BundleBoss

**The free plugin** is a complete fixed-bundle product. You choose the products, the quantities and the discount; customers configure every unit (a three t-shirt pack can be three different sizes and colours), and stock stays honest from the configurator through to a fulfilment pick list. Nothing is locked or crippled.

**The Builder (Pro)** turns a bundle into a configurator: customers pick each component from pools you define, guided by compatibility and quantity rules, with personalisation and logo uploads, shareable build links, a step-by-step layout and the size-run quantity grid. It is one add-on, enabled with a licence key, and the free plugin never depends on it.

The [pricing page](/pricing/) has the full free-versus-Pro breakdown.
