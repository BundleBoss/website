## Sale countdown

On the bundle's promotion settings, set a **start** and **end** date and time with the built-in date-time picker. The product page then shows a live **"Sale ending in"** countdown with a depleting progress bar, and it disappears on its own when the sale ends. Use it to add urgency to a limited offer without touching the price logic; the countdown is presentation, and your [pricing](/docs/pricing/) is what actually applies.

## "You might also like"

Hand-pick other bundles to show in a **carousel** below the product form. It is a simple way to move shoppers between related bundle pages, for example from a starter pack to a premium one, or between seasonal kits.

<div class="callout tip"><span class="callout-label">Tip</span><p>Pair a sale countdown with a [quantity discount tier](/docs/pricing/) so the offer rewards buying more, and the carousel to point shoppers at the next bundle once they have committed to one.</p></div>
