Common questions

Why pay when there are cheaper bundle plugins?
The cheap ones do fixed bundles. So does our free plugin. Pro is for the Builder: configurators with rules that stop customers buying combinations that do not work. The closest thing to it is WooCommerce's own Composite Products at £112 (about $150) a year — and its own reviewers say it has not shipped a meaningful feature in years.

How does this compare to the official extensions?
To get both halves of what BundleBoss does you would buy two separate WooCommerce.com extensions: Product Bundles (£60 a year) plus Composite Products (£112 a year) — £172 together, around $215. BundleBoss Solo gives you both halves, designed to work as one, for $99.

What happens if I cancel?
The plugin keeps working, your bundles stay intact, you stop receiving updates and support. Your data is never held hostage.

Is the free version crippled?
No. It covers what the official fixed-bundle extension charges £60 a year for — free, forever. Pro adds the Builder, not the basics.
