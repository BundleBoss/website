Before you ask

The questions we hear most.

Is the free version actually useful?

Yes. The free plugin is the complete fixed-bundle product: per-unit configuration, percentage or fixed pricing with quantity tiers, oversell-proof stock, the cart discount nudge, fulfilment pick lists, and full JSON export. Pro adds the Builder on top, it does not unlock the basics.

What do I get in Pro?

The Builder: choice slots, compatibility and quantity rules, the quantity-grid size runs, per-item personalisation with file upload, the full-page Configurator layout, build-a-box, gift mode, frequently bought together, buy X get Y, conditional items, shareable build links, the step-by-step layout, hero slots, deal badges in the chooser, and a one-click importer for WooCommerce Composite Products.

How is this different from Composite Products or other bundle plugins?

The official extensions, YITH and WPC are all capable. The difference is that matching BundleBoss usually means stitching two or three plugins together, and none of them let a customer buy several of one product and make each unit different. That per-unit run, plus personalisation in the same plugin, is what BundleBoss is built for. See the full comparison.

Will it slow my store down?

No. BundleBoss makes no external calls and builds on standard WooCommerce product, cart and order primitives. It has been profiled against a catalogue of 50,000 products.

Does it work with my theme, blocks and HPOS?

Yes. It is theme-agnostic, supports the Cart and Checkout Blocks and block (FSE) themes, works with High-Performance Order Storage, and is translation-ready with WPML and Polylang.

Can customers really configure each unit separately?

Yes, that is the whole idea. A three t-shirt pack can be three different sizes and colours, each chosen in a guided configurator and bought as a single cart item.

Is my data locked in?

No. Every bundle exports to one JSON file you can move between sites, and uninstalling never deletes your work unless you switch that on yourself.

When can I start using it?

Now. BundleBoss is live and free on WordPress.org. Search for it under Plugins, Add New in your dashboard, or get it at wordpress.org/plugins/bundle-boss.
